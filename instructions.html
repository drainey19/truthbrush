TruthBrush Instruction Manual

Parts:

  • Storage Tube and Cap
    • The storage tube is used to store your extra toothbrush heads, hold on to your toothbrush handle to provide an “all-in-one” toothbrush.
  • Toothbrush Handle
    • The handle to your toothbrush which will attach to the head via metal threads. A steel rod extends from the handle to fit into the bottom of the head.
  • Toothbrush Heads
    • Brush heads of various lengths and stiffness will be stored in the storage tube and screw onto the top of your handle.

Assembly

Assuming the TruthBrush is fully disassembled and the handle is connected to the storage tube:
  • Unscrew the cap to the storage tube, revealing multiple brush heads. Don't lose the cap!
  • Remove a single brush head and set it aside. Screw the cap back into place.
  • Remove the handle from the storage tube: Slide the handle upwards until free and set aside.
    • Note: Pulling the handle out of the storage tube arms instead of lifting upwards may damage the storage tube arms.
  • Hold on to the handle with one hand and one brush head with the other.
  • Align the brush head with the handle, making sure to insert the steel rod into the bottom of the brush head.
  • Rotating clockwise, screw the brush head onto the handle until tight.
  • Now you are ready to apply toothpaste and begin your journey to a healthy, fresh smile!

Disassemble/Store:

Option 1:
  • Slide the toothbrush, fully assembled, into the storage slot. Slide the handle downwards so that the plastic frame is not damaged.
Option 2:
  • Unscrew the brush head from the handle by rotating counterclockwise.
  • Set both the handle and head aside.
  • Unscrew the cap of the storage tube, making sure to set it in a safe place.
  • Drop the toothbrush head into the top of the tube, and screw the cap back on.
  • Slide handle downwards into the plastic storage tube arms.
  • Your TruthBrush is now safely disassembled and ready for travel or storage.
